How to Delete Your Data in AI Agent

Your privacy is important to us. If you'd like to delete your data, please follow the steps below:

🔄 Using the "Clear Chat" Feature

You can clear your chat history by tapping the “Clear Chat” button inside the app. This starts a new session and removes the conversation associated with the previous session from the app experience.

📧 Request Full Data Deletion

If you would like to request deletion of any server-side data associated with your account (or you are using a custom backend), contact us:

  • Email: zub165@yahoo.com
  • Support page: Support Center

Please include: the email used in the app (if any), your platform (iOS/Android/Web), and the date/time of your request.

🔒 Data Retention

The app is designed to minimize retention. Conversation data may be stored temporarily to provide the chat experience and then expires over time. If you request deletion, we will process it as quickly as possible subject to legal and operational requirements.
