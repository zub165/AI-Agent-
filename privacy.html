1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

  • Account Information (if you create an account): Email address and any other details you provide

  • Subscription Information: Purchase history and subscription status

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, app version
  • Usage Data: Feature usage, interaction patterns, error logs
  • Approximate Location: Country/region derived from device settings or IP (when available) to support language and compliance
  • Advertising Data (if ads are enabled): Advertising identifiers and ad performance signals as provided by your device/ads SDK

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide and improve our AI-powered medical assistance services
  • Generate HOPI notes and medical responses

  • Analyze app usage to improve functionality
  • Comply with legal obligations and enforce our terms

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share information in the following circumstances:

  • Service Providers: With vendors that help us operate the app (for example: cloud hosting, AI processing, crash reporting, analytics, ads, and payment/subscription platforms)
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect our rights
  • Content Moderation: Content reports may be shared with our moderation team
  • Business Transfers: In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets

AI Processing (OpenAI)

When you use the AI chat features, we send certain data to our backend server and to OpenAI (a third‑party AI provider) to generate responses.

Data sent for AI processing may include:

  • Chat content: the symptoms/questions you type (and any intake answers you submit)
  • Session identifier: a session_id used to keep your conversation together
  • Language code: your selected language (for example: en, es, ur, ar)

We require your permission before the first AI request is sent. You can review or reset this permission in the app at Settings → AI Data Sharing.
